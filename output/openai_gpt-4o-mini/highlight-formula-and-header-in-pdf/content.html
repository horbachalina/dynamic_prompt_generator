What Does Highlighting Formulas and Headers in PDF Involve?

Highlighting in pdfFiller involves using the Tools menu to select the Highlight option, allowing users to emphasize specific text within a PDF document. For instance, a financial analyst reviewing budget documents can quickly highlight key formulas to ensure they stand out for team discussions. Step-by-Step Guide to Highlighting Formulas and Headers in Your PDF

  1. Open your PDF document in pdfFiller.
  2. Click on the "Tools" menu located at the top of the screen.
  3. Select the "Highlight" option from the dropdown menu.
  4. Click and drag your cursor over the formula or header you wish to highlight.
  5. To undo any highlighting mistakes, use the "Undo" feature available in the toolbar.
  6. After highlighting, remember to save your document to retain the changes.

Managing Your Highlights After Placement in PDF

When sharing documents, highlights will appear to all recipients, ensuring that important sections are visible. Limitations of Highlighting in PDF Documents

While highlighting in PDF documents is a useful feature, there are some limitations to consider. For instance, highlighting may not function correctly on scanned PDFs, as the text may not be recognized properly. The Erase tool can remove highlights, but it may not always work on complex annotations. Additionally, highlights in shared documents are permanent, meaning that once they are saved, they cannot be easily undone without reverting to a previous version.
