7. H1: How to Cross Out Text in a PDF for Team Review

8. TL;DR ANSWER: To cross out text in a PDF for your team, upload the document to pdfFiller. Use the Draw tool (Tools → Draw) to freehand a line through the text, or the Line tool for a straight strikethrough. Share the document via a link or email invitation so team members can see the markups, add comments, and collaborate in real-time within the browser-based editor.
