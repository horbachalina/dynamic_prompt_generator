7.  **H1:** How to Highlight a Formula and Header in a PDF

8.  **TL;DR ANSWER:** To highlight a formula and header in a PDF using pdfFiller, upload your document and open it in the editor. Select the Highlight tool from the Tools menu, then click and drag over the text you want to emphasize. Use the Select tool to adjust the color or transparency of your highlights, distinguishing formulas from headers for clearer review before downloading or sharing the marked-up document.
